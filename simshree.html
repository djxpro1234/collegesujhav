Sydenham Institute of Management Studies, Research and Entrepreneurship Education (SIMSREE), Mumbai

Sydenham Institute of Management Studies, Research and Entrepreneurship Education (SIMSREE), Mumbai is one of India’s most prestigious government MBA colleges. Established in 1983, SIMSREE is affiliated with the University of Mumbai and is well known for its academic excellence, affordable fee structure, and strong industry connections.

SIMSREE Mumbai is a top preference among MBA/MMS aspirants due to its exceptional return on investment (ROI), experienced faculty, and consistent placement record across reputed national and multinational companies.

SIMSREE NRI / Management Quota Admission

SIMSREE Mumbai offers limited supernumerary seats under the Management and NRI quota. These seats are available for NRI, OCI/PIO, and foreign national candidates.

Admission through the NRI quota involves lower competition and is based on academic background and eligibility criteria set by the institute.
