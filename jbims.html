Jamnalal Bajaj Institute of Management Studies (JBIMS), Mumbai

Jamnalal Bajaj Institute of Management Studies, Mumbai is one of the top management colleges in India. JBIMS Mumbai was established in 1965 and affiliated with University of Mumbai. JBIMS Mumbai is famously known as CEO factory of India because a significant number of its alumni have achieved CEO positions in various major corporations, including notable figures like Uday Kotak (Kotak Mahindra Bank), Ajay Piramal (Piramal Group), and Natarajan Chandrasekaran (Tata Sons).

JBIMS Mumbai is a top choice for MBA aspirants due to its reputation, industry connections, and strong ROI.

JBIMS NRI Quota Admission

Some supernumerary seats are reserved in JBIMS Mumbai for OCI/PIO, Foreign Students, Children of Indian Workers in Gulf Countries (CIWGC) and Non-Resident Indian (NRI). Students can enroll in JBIMS through these seats.

Taking admission through the NRI quota is slightly easier due to lower competition.
