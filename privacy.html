Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 27, 2025

At CollegeSujhav, accessible from https://collegesujhav.in, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and your rights related to that information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Messages you send us (e.g. for newsletters, or contact us, etc.)
  • Educational preferences
  • Location

Non-Personal Information

  • Browser type
  • IP address
  • Pages visited
  • Time on site
  • Referral source

This information is collected from users when it is shared with us via site usage or contact form and similar sources.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your queries and messages
  • Providing college admission guidance (based on user data)
  • Sending updates, newsletters (only when permitted)
  • Improving the website experience and service quality
  • Analyzing website usage to enhance our content

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Measure website performance
  • Deliver personalized content
  • Enhance user experience

You can choose to disable cookies via your browser settings, though this may affect your ability to use some features of the site.

4. Third-Party Services

We may integrate or link to third-party services like:

  • Google Analytics
  • Google Ads
  • Social media plugins (e.g., Meta, LinkedIn)

These services may collect data as per their privacy policies. We encourage you to review their privacy terms before interacting with them.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what information we collect and how we use it
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent for data use

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@collegesujhav.in.

7. Children’s Privacy

College Sujahv does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided us information, please contact us for removal.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us:

Email: support@collegesujhav.in

Website: www.collegesujhav.in
